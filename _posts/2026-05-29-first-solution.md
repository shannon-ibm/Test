title: "Reducing Data Risk in Hybrid Environments"
date: 2026-05-29
---

## The situation
A healthcare client was struggling to track sensitive data across hybrid systems.

## What made it tricky
- Data spread across cloud + on-prem
- No clear ownership
- Compliance pressure increasing

## The approach
We focused on:
- Data discovery and classification
- Centralized visibility
- Policy enforcement

## What changed
- Faster audit readiness
- Reduced exposure risk
- Clear ownership established

## Takeaways
Start with visibility. You can’t protect what you can’t see.
